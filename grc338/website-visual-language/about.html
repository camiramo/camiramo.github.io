Luna Red is located in the heart of downtown San Luis Obispo in a Spanish style building situated over the San Luis Obispo Creek. Our sprawling terra cotta tiled patio offers guests a unique opportunity to dine beside the Picturesque San Luis Obispo Mission de Tolosa Bell Tower. Our lively gathering spot and globally inspired menu of handcrafted dishes like authentic Spanish paella, craft tacos, and delicious shareable plates create a culinary experience like no other in SLO. Luna Red has become known for our weekly special events such as happy hour and Taco Tuesday, which keep our guests coming back for more.
